Welcome to LTA!

Welcome to Lavrisha Tech and Associates! LTA is a technology oriented consulting firm that is based in downtown Cleveland, OH. We are local based and strive to help both people and small business in the Cleveland area.

What we do:

LTA's specialists can help with; on-site service of computers, design personalized web sites, and consult on all types of electronic marketing and technology.

Three types of main jobs:

Type 1: websites that we make

LTA builds unique websites that are personalized for what the customer needs. We provide specifically; personalized designs, unique color schemes, and special functions that online website builders can't provide.

Type 2: on site services

Our tech specialists can provide local service to both computers and servers. We can remove viruses (by application and by hand) and replace parts that have broken.

Type 3: consulting services

Our consultants keep track of all innovations regarding technology, specifically trends in social media and new ways to market online. We help provide a path to a better way to market your company.
